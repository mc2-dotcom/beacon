Section 10 · Methodology & Acknowledged Gaps

Honest about what Beacon can and can't see.

Evidence tiers are stated explicitly. The agents' final report will only cite frame classifications at the tier the underlying evidence supports.

TierDefinitionUse for
VerifiedDirect primary text in Beacon KB with confirmed timestamp and engagement countAll 9 quoted artifacts; tweet-share statistics
StrongMulti-source corroboration including primary text plus cross-ecosystem reportingNetwork touchpoints (CodePink, BTN, People's Forum); trip dates
ModerateSingle-source secondary reporting (e.g., Fox News describing Yale speech) without primary transcriptYale speech "paper tigers" quote; Tiananmen square remarks
InferentialPattern-level claims requiring agent synthesis across multiple data pointsTopical-shift directionality; counter-evidence absence

Acknowledged data gaps the agents will work around or flag.

  1. No Twitch transcripts post-2022. The "Hasanabi Stream VOD Audio" feed in Beacon has 56 episodes — all from July–September 2022. The "CENTURY OF HUMILIATION" stream (Nov 12, 2025) and all trip / post-trip stream content cannot be audited from Beacon directly. Agent 5 will attempt YouTube timestamp retrieval for the Yale speech and key streams.
  2. No PRC state-media corpus in Beacon. Global Times, CGTN, Xinhua, China Daily are not ingested. The cosine-similarity centroid methodology used in the AJ+/MB study cannot be replicated. Agent 5 will fetch external corroboration; Agent 1 will use lexical / frame-based classification instead.
  3. No control-group left streamers. Vaush, David Pakman, Briahna Joy Gray, Majority Report not in Beacon. Sam Seder has only 61 tweets from Jan 2026. The U.S.-left baseline must be partial. Agent 3 will use the available podcast ecosystem as a minimal control.
  4. Singham-network social data starts December 2025. CodePink (1,001 posts) and BTN (2,373 posts) datasets begin too late to compare network amplification of Piker pre-trip vs. post-trip from Beacon data alone.
  5. News DB starts July 2025. Adequate for the study period; cannot establish longer historical baselines for media coverage of Piker.
  6. Yale speech (Apr 15, 2026) is in coverage, not corpus. Direct quotes ("paper tigers," "fascist death spiral") appear in Fox News reporting; full transcript not in Beacon. Cited at Moderate tier unless Agent 5 obtains primary recording.
