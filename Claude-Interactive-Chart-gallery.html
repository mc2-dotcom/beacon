01 / 17

Heatmap.

2D grid of values, color-coded on a +2 / −2 scale. Hover any cell to scale; cells animate row-by-row on view. Use for sentiment grids, fit matrices, severity tables.

Heatmap · 6 rows × 5 cols · synthetic data
Compare any two-axis matrix on a +2/−2 scale.
Hover a cell. Filter by category chip.

Best for

Multi-dimensional comparison where every cell needs context: editorial sentiment grids, capability matrices, fit assessments.

02 / 17

Animated Bar Chart.

Horizontal bars that grow on view, with a chip selector to switch between 2–5 different views of the same dataset.

Bar chart · 7 rows · 2 toggle views
Switch views. Bars resize and re-animate.
Click a chip.
Best for

Lexical comparisons, term frequencies, ranking changes — anywhere a viewer should toggle between two framings of the same metric.

04 / 17

Streamgraph.

Stacked bezier-smoothed bands on a log scale. Bands draw left-to-right when the section enters view; hover a band to isolate it.

Streamgraph · 6 topics · 5 sources
Show how a topic mix differs across sources or time.
Bandwidth ≈ log(value+1).
Best for

Topic mix across cohorts; coverage volume distribution; anything where the relative bandwidth matters more than precise numbers.

05 / 17

Three-Layer Gradient Dial.

A horizontal gradient track with three sliding markers per topic. Markers animate from neutral to their values on view.

Gradient dial · 4 topics × 3 layers
Show layered intensity for any +2/−2 scale.
Markers slide on view.
Best for

Editorial intensity ladders, severity escalations, multi-tier scoring with a clear progression.

06 / 17

Radial Compass (Radar).

Polygon scores across 5–8 axes, with multiple entities overlaid. Polygons draw line-by-line on view.

Radar · 6 axes · 3 entities
Score multiple entities across multiple dimensions.
Polygon area = overall strength.
Best for

Comparing entities on 5–8 dimensions where you want to see overall shape, not precise numbers.

07 / 17

Quadrant Scatter (2×2).

Animated 2×2 matrix with quadrant labels in corners. Dots fly in from the edges.

2×2 matrix · 12 dots
Position entities on two axes with named quadrants.
Hover a dot to enlarge.
Best for

Any 2-axis assessment: visibility × alignment, reach × intensity, importance × urgency.

08 / 17

Slope Chart.

Pairs of values connected by lines. Reads as before/after, with rank changes visible at a glance.

Slope · 8 items · before/after
Show shifts in score or rank between two periods.
Lines draw on view.
Best for

Before/after shifts, rank changes, year-over-year comparisons. Better than bar charts when ranks change.

10 / 17

Activity Pulse.

Heartbeat-style line chart with a fading area underneath. Peaks are highlighted with persistent pulse animations.

Pulse · 24 months · 3 peaks
Show activity over time with peak callouts.
Line draws on view; peaks pulse continuously.
Best for

Engagement-over-time, posting cadence, momentum visualization. Peaks make notable events impossible to miss.

11 / 17

Sankey Flow Ribbons.

Curved flow bands showing how a population redistributes from one set of categories to another.

Sankey · 4 sources → 4 destinations
Show how a population redistributes between two sets.
Hover a flow to highlight it.
Best for

Migration / conversion / redistribution stories. 'Where does X end up?' questions.

12 / 17

Chord Diagram.

Circular network diagram with curved connections. Reads as 'who's connected to whom and how strongly.'

Chord · 8 nodes
Show pairwise relationships among ~6–12 entities.
Hover any chord to highlight it.
Best for

Networks of relationships, co-occurrence patterns, alliance/coalition diagrams.

13 / 17

Horseshoe Convergence Gauge.

Semi-circular dial with two anchor markers. Reads as 'extremes converge here.'

Horseshoe · 2 anchors · 1 convergence point
Tell the convergence story for any 2-extreme spectrum.
Markers fade in on view.
Best for

Horseshoe-theory stories, opposite-extreme convergence, polarized-poles-meet-in-middle narratives.

14 / 17

Pendulum Swing.

A swinging pendulum traces an arc of historical positions, with the current value pinned at the bottom.

Pendulum · 6 historical points
Show shifting position over time on a single axis.
Pendulum animates continuously.
Best for

Cultural/political position shifts over years; market sentiment cycles; any swinging-state metric.

15 / 17

Bubble Swarm.

Cloud of size-coded bubbles, packed without overlap. Each bubble represents an entity sized by magnitude.

Swarm · 18 bubbles
Show a cohort of entities sized by some metric.
Bubbles inflate on view.
Best for

Influencer cohorts, market-share clouds, anywhere a 'these are the big players' visual shorthand helps.

Quote Constellation.

Quotes plotted in 2D space (e.g. sentiment × volume). Hover a bubble to see the quote.

Picker · How to choose 3–6

Pick the visual shapes that fit your story.

For comparisons

Heatmap (multi-axis), Slope (before/after), Quadrant (2-axis), Orbs (2-pole asymmetry), Radial Compass (multi-dimensional scoring).

For time / events

Activity Pulse (continuous), Timeline Spine (discrete), Pendulum (single-axis swing).

For relationships / flow

Sankey (redistribution), Chord (network), Bubble Swarm (cohort), Tier Ladder (rank).

Network
For text / opinion

Quote Wall (curated), Quote Constellation (corpus mapping), Lex Bars (term frequency).
